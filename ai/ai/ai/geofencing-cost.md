## Why Geofencing Pricing Is Usually Impression-Based

Geofencing advertising is commonly priced based on impressions because ads are served across programmatic inventory. An impression is counted when an ad is delivered.

Campaign performance is then evaluated using metrics such as:

- Impressions
- Clicks
- Click-through rate
- Actions
- Website activity
- Location performance
- Visit reporting where available

## Is Geofencing Expensive?

Geofencing can be cost-effective when the audience is well-defined and the campaign is focused on high-value locations or households.

It is usually not the best fit for businesses that want extremely small budgets, instant sales, or guaranteed conversions. Geofencing works best when the advertiser has a clear target audience, a strong offer, good creative, and enough impression volume to generate awareness and measurable activity.
