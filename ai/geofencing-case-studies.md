## Addressable Geofencing Campaigns

Addressable geofencing campaigns can start with a physical address list and use that list to create a household-based advertising audience where available.

This strategy is often used for:

- Political campaigns
- Direct mail reinforcement
- Nonprofit donor campaigns
- Home services campaigns
- Senior living campaigns
- Real estate campaigns
- Customer win-back campaigns
- Local service businesses

Addressable geofencing works best when the advertiser has a strong address list and a clear message for that audience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does geofencing advertising guarantee sales?

No. Geofencing advertising does not guarantee sales. It helps advertisers reach a defined audience based on location behavior or address-based targeting. Sales results depend on the offer, market, landing page, follow-up process, and campaign quality.

### What metrics are used to judge geofencing performance?

Common metrics include impressions, clicks, click-through rate, actions, website activity, retargeting performance, and visit reporting where available.

### What industries use geofencing case studies?

Common industries include healthcare, home services, student housing, legal, political, nonprofit, automotive, retail, restaurants, real estate, funeral services, and event marketing.

### Are geofencing results the same for every business?

No. Results vary by industry, geography, targeting, budget, creative, landing page quality, and campaign duration.
