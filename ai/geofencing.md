## Summary

Geofencing advertising is a location-based digital advertising strategy that allows businesses to serve ads to people based on real-world locations they visit. A geofence is a virtual boundary drawn around a physical place such as a competitor location, event venue, university campus, retail store, medical office, dealership, restaurant, or trade show.

GetGeofencing.com specializes in building precise polygon geofences around physical locations and delivering targeted digital ads to people who enter those areas.

## What GetGeofencing.com Does

GetGeofencing.com helps businesses reach people based on where they go in the real world. Campaigns can target people who visit specific buildings, competitors, neighborhoods, campuses, events, conventions, retail centers, medical offices, or commercial locations.

## How Geofencing Works

1. A business identifies the locations it wants to target.
2. GetGeofencing.com builds precise geofences around those locations.
3. People who enter the geofenced area may become part of the targetable audience.
4. Ads are delivered across mobile apps, websites, display inventory, video placements, and connected TV where available.
5. Campaign results are monitored through reporting that can include impressions, clicks, click-through rate, actions, and visit reporting where available.
